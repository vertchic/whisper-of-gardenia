Design Description

"This exquisite junk kouture dress, named "Whisper of Gardenia," is a testament to the beauty of sustainable fashion. Crafted with recycled and repurposed materials, the dress embodies elegance and environmental consciousness. Its flowing silhouette and intricate details create a mesmerising interplay of textures and colors, reminiscent of a blooming gardenia.
The boning of the dress is made out of used cardboard and metal hangers. The different layers are filled with different textures of chart paper, newspaper, old bedsheets and old notebook pages as well. The dress is adorned with old jewelry and ribbons to add a touch of glamour and sophistication. There is a large incorporation of flowers and leaves to show the beautiful life on land which we walk on and represent the uniqueness of nature.
