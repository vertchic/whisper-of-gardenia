Introduction

We are Vert Chic, a sustainability-centered and environmental brand, that focuses on designing elegant and chic regalia that not only help you look your best, but also help the environment and spread awareness of the current affairs regarding climate change and other occurrences in our own, creative way.
Our Vision is to redefine luxury by intertwining creativity, sustainability, and climate consciousness.
Join us as we aim to bring our creations to life, and help bring life back into our planet.



Our Story

Our design and objectives for making the dress focus on SDG 15- Life on Land, which is aiming towards protecting, restoring and promoting sustainable use of terrestrial ecosystems, sustainably manage forests, combat desertification, and halt and reverse land degradation and halt biodiversity loss.
